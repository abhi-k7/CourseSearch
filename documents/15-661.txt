Title: 15-661Interaction and Expression using the Pausch Bridge Lighting:
Course Number: 15-661
Units: 4 units
Prerequisites: None
Corequisites: None
Description: Working in cross-disciplinary teams, students will explore light as art, interactive design and programming using a Pharos lighting control system. Students will explore the use of light and interaction using the actual controls within the Randy Pausch Memorial Bridge. Student teams will develop final projects that will be exhibited on the actual Randy Pausch Memorial Bridge.
